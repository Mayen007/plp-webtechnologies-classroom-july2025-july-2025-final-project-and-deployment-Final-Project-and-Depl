Commission Information

Custom Artwork

I accept commissions for original paintings and mixed media pieces. Each commission is uniquely created to reflect your vision and space.

  • Personal consultation
  • Custom size and medium
  • Progress updates
  • 3-6 week timeline

Process

  1. Initial Consultation: Discuss your vision, space, and preferences
  2. Concept Development: Create sketches and propose ideas
  3. Agreement: Finalize details and timeline
  4. Creation: Begin artwork with regular updates
  5. Completion: Final approval and delivery

Investment

Commission pricing varies based on size, medium, and complexity. Please contact me for a personalized quote.

Starting prices:

  • Small (up to 16" x 20"): $800+
  • Medium (up to 24" x 30"): $1,500+
  • Large (30" x 40"+): $2,500+
